Projects

Projects and tools I happens to maintain or create:

  • avatar²: A framework for reversing and analysing embedded firmware.
  • Sirocco: A Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Intrusion Detection System (IDS) on Zephyr RTOS designed to detect low-level radio attacks.
  • Boomerang: A Rust library that facilitates Linux system call instrumentation.
  • Chestburster: A tool for rehosting running Linux process between different kernels, such as moving a process from a physical device to an emulator.
