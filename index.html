I am Paul L. R. OLIVIER, a postdoctoral researcher in the Tolérance aux fautes et Sureté de Fonctionnement (TSF) team at LAAS-CNRS, collaborating with Vincent NICOMETTE and Guillaume AURIOL.

Previously, I completed my Ph.D under the supervision of Aurélien Francillon at Software and Systems Security group of EURECOM in Sophia-Antipolis (France). I hold a Master’s degree in Telecommunication and Networking from ENSEEIHT in Toulouse, France (2019) and a Post Master’s degree in Security in Systems and Communication from EURECOM.

My interests focus on system security, particularly program analysis and the security of embedded systems. I also appreciate problematics related to reproducibility in computer science and research.

I am one of the maintainer of the avatar² project, an analysis and reversing framework for embedded firmware.
